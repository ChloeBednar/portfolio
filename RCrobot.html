Turf Wars Robot

Project Overview

This was the final project for my Computer-Aided Machine Design course, in which I worked with a group of four other engineering students to design a robot to compete in a class competition called Turf Wars. We named our robot Tony Robo and placed 3rd in competiton. I was the lead design engineer, so I modelled each of our design iterations in SolidWorks.

Turf Wars Final Robot

CNC Milling Delrin Component

Constraints

The main constraints of the project resources, footprint, and manufacturing methods. We were tasked with building our robot from components in which we utilized each of the manufacturing processes we learned throughout the course (3D printing, CNC milling, silicone molding, laser engraving, lathe, bandsaw, drill press) on at least one component.
